Coronavirus in Nigeria hits over 7000!!!

Posted by Jibril Abdullahi on May 22nd, 2020

Coronavirus has been on an increasing alarming rate over the past few weeks. Few will wonder why the surge in the number because the country have been on lockdown since the index case was discovered in Lagos. The fact is that Coronavirus takes just a single person to spread to a thousand.

Number of tests carried out by the Nigerian Center for Diseases Control, NCDC, has improved over the last couple of weeks. The covid19 has hit Nigeria on a high rate, abeit with more tests to be conducted. As at today, 22nd May, 2020, data available shows that Nigeria currently have over 7000 covid 19 cases sinces its index case.

339 new cases of COVID-19 have just been recorded across the country, bringing Nigerias infection to 7016.

The Nigeria Centre for Disease Control, made the announcement on Twitter, with Lagos having the highest number per state.
